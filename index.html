Pricing Prophet
AI pricing auditor for SaaS & B2B

02
Live App AI Tool
BoKay AI
Property listing optimizer for the Spanish market

"Bo kay" means home in Martinican Creole. Because a good listing doesn't just describe a property — it makes someone feel like they're already home.

Idealista listings are notoriously poor — too short, missing key details, no structure. BoKay AI takes your raw property details (rooms, size, energy rating, photos) and generates a compelling enhanced description that actually sells. Built for the Spanish market, where a bad listing costs you thousands in sale price or months on the market.

Next: URL import — paste your Idealista listing directly, no manual entry needed.

03
EdTech Live App
Multiplication Master
Multiplication practice that feels like play

Passive memorization without practice doesn't stick — so I built something that makes the practice feel like play.

Two modes: Learn and Play. The learning grid lets kids hover to see multiplication as area — rows, columns, and rectangles lighting up to make the concept visual. The play mode offers three games: Flashcards for self-paced recall, Quiz Whiz with streak tracking and confetti, and Time Attack — 60 seconds, as many problems as possible.

Built for my kids. Probably useful for yours too.

Pricing Source of Truth
Single source of truth for pricing across markets and languages

Managing pricing tables across multiple markets means multiple spreadsheets, multiple owners, and inevitable drift. Wrong prices go live. Translations lag. Eligibility rules get lost in email threads.

One source of truth for all pricing data, with per-market eligibility rules, locale management, and auto-translation via Gemini. Any team — product, marketing, legal — works from the same table. Built as a prototype for stakeholders at my current company.
